For parents & guardians

A smoother morning
for everyone.

A few simple steps keep arrival safe, punctual and stress-free for every family.

◷ School gate window8:00 AM to 8:30 AM
  1. 01
    Arrive on time

    Please come to school within the gate window. Plan a few extra minutes for traffic.

  2. 02
    Dress appropriately

    Please arrive in proper attire. Shorts and nightwear are not permitted on campus.

  3. 03
    Keep the gate clear

    Park bikes and cars at least 100 metres from campus to reduce congestion during drop-off.

Visitor policy

Every visitor must report to security.

Please stop at the security desk, state the purpose of your visit and follow the entry process before proceeding into campus.
